-I decided on a set amount of strokes being the scoring system, with the personal best being kept.

-I made pits, varying terrain, moving walls, lower friction areas (water), and bounce pads. I did not make a moving platform, or have time to figure out portals. 

-The vision I had with grass,sand, dirt was changed to being more of a cave environment. So I used a stone, golf ball, stalactite, and mushroom assets for most of it. The background was a bunch of assets to make it look like a cave. The water was just a blue that I made transparent and for the waterfall I put a cloud at the bottom to look like mist.

-I kept the same controls with the addition of camera panning by holding w,a,s or d to pan the camera in that direction. 

-I made a starting/ending UI. With the option to reset the level at any point and reset the high score at any point. Also a stroke counter in the top right to keep track.

-I did have sounds for different actions like hitting the ball, the ball bouncing, 3 winning sounds depending on how well the player did, entering water, hitting a mushroom, and button sounds for the reset buttons. The sfx for the ball shot and bounce are also changed depending on strength, so a harder hit would be a louder sound. The charging sound was also pitched up/down depending on how strong the shot was and set to repeat.

-I also made it so the only checks for if you could shoot the ball was a grounded check and a vertical velocity check. I thought this kept it more dynamic and let the player correct mistakes if they were quick.
